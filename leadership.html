Women In Computing

I am an officer for the University of Utah's Women in Computing club. I have been an Officer for 3 years and have helped the club plan numerous company sponsored and school events. I am currently the Treasurer and help the club with funding and fundraising. I joined WIC because I wanted to find a community of women in CS. I am passionate about expanding WIC and using it as a safe space for all femme identifying students in computing at the U.

Utah Center for Inclusive Computing

I am an ambassador the the Utah Center for Inclusive computing. We help cultivate a community for underepresented groups in Computing in Utah. As an ambassador I help plan events, table and do outreach at local high schools and community colleges. Our mission is to get more diverse students involved in computing and support them in their journeys.

GGV Next Gen Fellows

I am a member of the 2023 GGV next gen fellows summer intern cohort. As a fellow I participate in weekely career driven meetings, meant to help underrepresnted groups gain knowledge about career growth and create lasting connections. Fellows also receive access to a world-class learning and development curriculum that focuses on entrepreneurship, design thinking, and getting comfortable with failure as a way to grow.
